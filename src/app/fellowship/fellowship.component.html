About the Program

In addition to the most comprehensive neuromodulator and dermal filler training available in Canada, learners now have the opportunity to undergo supervised clinical training over a period of three months. Those who complete the fellowship and accomplish all learning objectives will be the most qualified trainees entering the medical aesthetic industry today.

This one of a kind program is offered in Edmonton, Kelowna and Vancouver. Learners will be exposed to high volume clinics and be immersed in an incredibly high yield learning environment. Working with experts, seeing how large clinics run, learning about other aesthetic services, seeing the different technologies available, and having complete access to our experts make The Canadian Fellowship of Cosmetic Injectables an invaluable experience.

An in depth look at The Fellowship Program

Fellowship Program Overview

Becoming an expert in any career takes time, practice and oversight. Aesthetics Canada© recognizes this. The botox and filler courses provides the most comprehensive training available in Canada. At the botox course, learners have the opportunity to inject many cosmetic treatment areas and perform therapeutic treatments as well. The hands-on experience provided at these courses is extensive and thousands of units of neuromodulators are utilized to achieve this. Hence our learners can leave and produce natural results and ensure patient safety. But our educators queried how to take this learning experience to the next level, and created the Canadian Fellowship of Cosmetic Injectables.

Our Fellowship Program is designed to give medical professionals guaranteed longitudinal clinical experience. It guarantees clinical experience under the direct supervision of industry leaders including surgeons, specialists, GPs, and nurse experts. This program allows learners the chance to see how clinics operate behind the scenes. It ensures learners develop good before and after photos as part of a complete treatment portfolio. It provides job and networking opportunities while building confidence, safe technique and skill. This program is revolutionizing the industry and delivers the highest level of learning available in medical aesthetics. Applications will be accepted and interviews with the applicable campus will be scheduled.
